## Cancellation

<Steps>
  <Step>
   Go to our website **https://lagless.gg/ ** and log in to the **Client Area**
   ![Client log in](/images/billing/client_area_login.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Click on **Services**
   ![Client panel services](/images/billing/services.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Click on the service you want to cancel, for example, the Minecraft service in the image below.
   ![Client products](/images/billing/my-products.png)  
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Click on **Request Cancellation** on the left.
   ![Request Cancellation](/images/billing/request_cancellation.png)  
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Fill in why you want to cancel and when, **end of billing** or **immediate**. When you click on the red **Request cancellation** button it's marked for cancellation.
   ![Request Cancellation](/images/billing/request_cancellation2.png) 
  </Step>
  <Step>
   You will receive feedback about your cancellation.
   ![Cancellation Submitted](/images/billing/cancellation-submitted.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
   When you go back to your service, you will see an **outstanding cancellation**.
   ![Service Cancellation](/images/billing/service-cancellation.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Didn't mean to cancel? you can still **Cancel Cancellation Request** by scrolling down the service you want to **Cancel Cancellation Request**
   ![Cancel Cancellation](/images/billing/cancel_cancellation.png) 
  </Step>
</Steps>
