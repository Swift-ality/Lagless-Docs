## Upgrading/Downgrading

To upgrade or downgrade your service, first log into the client area. As shown below.

![Client login](/images/website/client_login.png)  

After you have logged in.

1. Click on Services
2. Then click on the service you want to change.

![Client panel services](/images/billing/services.png)

From here click on the upgrade button.

![Upgrade Service(/images/billing/upgrade.png)  

From here you can choose one of the many upgrades available.

![Select upgrade](/images/billing/select_upgrade.png)  

<Callout title="resource unavailable" type="info">
  If in a rare case resources are not available on the machine. just make a ticket, and we will take care of it.
</Callout>
